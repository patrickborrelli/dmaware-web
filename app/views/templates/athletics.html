Athletics (Strength) - a check of your athletics ability covers difficult situations you encounter when climbing, jumping, or swimming. All athletics checks are based on your Strength modifier. Examples include:
  • You attemt to climb a sheer or slippery cliff, avoid hazards while scaling a wall, or cling to the surface while something is trying to knock you off.
  • You try to jump an unusually long distance or pull off a stunt mid-jump.
  • You struggle to swim or stay afloat in treacherous currents, storm-tossed waves, or areas of thick seaweed, or another creature tries to push or pull you underwater or otherwise interfere with your swimming.
